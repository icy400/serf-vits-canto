SERF-VITS-Canto Demo

论文摘要

Despite rapid progress in emotional speech synthesis, current TTS systems still lack fine-grained, controllable emotion, often misalign intended affect with realized prosody, and exhibit unstable pitch, a challenge amplified in complex tonal languages such as Cantonese. We present SERF-VITS-Canto, a VITS-based Cantonese TTS that integrates a spherical emotion representation with frame-level prosodic modeling. The method jointly predicts pitch and energy and fuses them with the emotion representation as conditioning signals throughout the generation pathway, aligning expressiveness with rhythmic realization while preserving speaker characteristics. Experiments on a Cantonese corpus indicate improved naturalness, stronger emotion controllability, more consistent relations between emotion and prosody, and greater pitch stability for Cantonese emotional TTS.
